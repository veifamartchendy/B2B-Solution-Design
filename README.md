# 🏨 B2B Technology Solution Design — Smart Hotel Digitalization

> A comprehensive B2B digital transformation proposal for a mid-to-luxury hotel, designed using Telkom Group's enterprise technology ecosystem. Achieved **Top 9 Honorable Mention** at Digistar Class B2B Pitch Competition, PT Telkom Indonesia (2025).

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was developed for the **Digistar Class B2B Pitch Competition** by PT Telkom Indonesia (2025) — a national-level competition where participants design real technology solution proposals for actual business clients.

**Client:** Mandala Nusantara Hotel (fictional mid-to-luxury heritage hotel in Jakarta)
**Challenge:** Design an end-to-end digital transformation roadmap to solve operational inefficiencies, connectivity gaps, data security risks, and revenue diversification needs.
**Team:** B2B Group 6-2 (5 members) | **My Role: Director of Sales & Marketing**
**Result:** 🏆 Top 9 Honorable Mention out of all participating teams

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Mandala Nusantara Hotel — a luxury business hotel targeting BUMN, MNCs, and diplomatic clients — faced four critical risks threatening its "Smart Heritage" vision:

| # | Pain Point | Business Impact |
|---|-----------|----------------|
| 1 | Unstable connectivity & limited bandwidth | Failed video conferences, poor premium client experience |
| 2 | Fragmented operations & manual processes | Long check-in queues, inconsistent service delivery |
| 3 | Inadequate data security infrastructure | Risk of breach affecting high-profile government clients |
| 4 | Revenue over-dependence on government events | Vulnerability to Inpres No. 1/2025 budget cuts |

---

## 💡 Solution Architecture

We proposed a **3-layer Smart Heritage Experience** powered by Telkom Group's ecosystem:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│         LAYER 3: Signature Guest Experience          │
│   IPTV · Smart Room IoT · Digital Concierge App     │
│              OCA Omni-channel CRM                    │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│       LAYER 2: Intelligent Operations                │
│   Antares IoT · Netmonk · Telkomsigma PMS           │
│         Qiscus Internal Chat · AI Analytics          │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│         LAYER 1: Core Infrastructure                 │
│   Dedicated Fiber · Telkomsel 5G · Flou Cloud       │
│      Managed Security · Indibiz Data Center         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## 🔍 Key Frameworks Applied

- **Pain Point → Solution Mapping** — structured problem identification to technology recommendation
- **TAM / SAM / SOM** — bottom-up market sizing using BPS & industry data
- **PESTEL Analysis** — macro-environment assessment for strategic positioning
- **3-Layer Architecture Design** — infrastructure → operations → guest experience
- **Financial Modelling** — revenue streams, OPEX breakdown, BEP projection
- **Competitive Landscape Analysis** — benchmarking against Dharmawangsa, AYANA, Hotel Borobudur
